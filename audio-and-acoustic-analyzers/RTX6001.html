Introduction

RTX6001 is a PC based 24-bit 192kSPS 90kHz high-performance audio analyzer with dual-channel balanced inputs and outputs, an isolated USB port, extremely low distortions and noises, and an extremely flat frequency response. It has been seamlessly integrated with the Multi-Instrument® software to form a completed multi-function high-performance audio analyzer.
